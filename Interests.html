My Hobbies and Interests

My interests can be broken down into two main parts: finance and tech.

My interest in finance largely stems from my love of entrepreneurship and investing. Over the past two years, I have been reading investment articles and books such as A Random Walk Down Wall Street, and I actively manage a small portfolio as a hobby. Recently, I have been especially concerned with the ESG aspects of companies, for which I often use MSCI as an index. To further understand the fundamentals of businesses as well as the process of investing in one, I am interning with MetaProp NYC. My roles include deal sourcing, financial diligence, and investor/LP database design.

For me, ML is an exciting interdisciplinary study that utilizes both my computer science and applied math skills.
